Anabolic steroids are substances that can disturb the natural production of hormones in the body, causing imbalances. These imbalances can trigger side effects like acne, hair loss, and mood changes.

The liver processes anabolic steroids during metabolism. Using them for a long time can harm this important organ. This damage can lead to severe health problems like liver cancer and liver failure.

Cardiovascular problems

Anabolic steroids can cause harm to the heart and blood vessels. They can increase the chances of having high blood pressure, heart attacks, and strokes. These effects are serious and can negatively impact a person's health.

Long-term use of anabolic steroids can create a situation where the body becomes reliant on them, leading to dependence. In many countries, anabolic steroids are classified as controlled substances. It is illegal to have or use them without a doctor's prescription. If someone uses them without medical supervision, they can face legal problems.
